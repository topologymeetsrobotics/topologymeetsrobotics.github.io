Call for Papers

The call for papers will be posted here after the workshop gets accepted. We will be soliciting contributions in a variety of topics including (but not limited to) the ones below.

Topics of Interest

  • Software packages/libraries with topological tools
  • Topological path/motion/task planning.
  • Simplicial representations and homological methods for sensor networks.
  • Applications of topological data analysis to robotics.
  • Topological representations for motion/behavior/trajectory prediction.
  • Topological representations for multirobot and human-robot systems.
  • Topological complexity analysis.
  • Interpretable/explainable AI
  • Human-friendly abstractions of complex systems.
  • Metrics for robotics using topological tools.
  • Representations for formal verification.
  • Geometric and topological methods for machine learning.
  • Topological representations for manipulation.
  • Topological SLAM.

Format of Contributions

We will be soliciting contributions in two different formats: a) short papers describing relevant prior or ongoing research (up to 4 pages); b) abstracts describing open problems (up to a page).
